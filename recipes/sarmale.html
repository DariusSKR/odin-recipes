Sarmale

Description:

Sarmale or Romanian cabbage rolls are a Romanian food recipe made and served during celebratory times. It's a staple in Romanian cuisine and can be made vegetarian as well. Sarmale are cabbage rolls filled with rice and buckwheat. They are filling, healthy and super delicious.

Ingredients:

  • Vegetable oil
  • Onion
  • Ground pork
  • Fresh parsley & drill
  • Salt & pepper
  • Sour cabbage leaves
  • Bacon
  • Tomato juice
  • Long grain rice

Steps for Romanian cabbage rolls:

  1. Prepare the filling
  2. Roll the filling
  3. Assemble the rolls
  4. Bake the rolls
